Welcome to our online resturant! Here in group 1, we're excited to offer you the meals that have stuck with us through the ages, gourmet cuisine that transports you to a different place with a single bite, dishes that inspire us. Food can bring anybody together. You might not like your racist uncle, but you both love cheesy potatoes.

Basically I thought it'd be super easy to just make webpages about our favourite foods and make it like we can actually order them at a resturant. AdminView would offer things like

  • Summary of all products sold
  • Total revenue view for day, week, month, year
  • Add/delete products
  • Easy price changes for flash promotions
  • Loyalty Program functions
    • give customer points
    • Enable extra points when certain things are bought
CustomerView would offer things like
  • Order History
  • Convenient "buy again" button
  • Edit a previous order and sumbit (say you wanted the same combo but a different pop)
  • Payment Information
  • Loyalty Program Overview
A menu would always be available for viewing and you don't need to sign in to place an order. Signing in however gives you loyalty points.

We would use a database like mySQL to store customer information. We can take "payments" through Square.
